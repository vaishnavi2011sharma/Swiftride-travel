Places to Visit in Coimbatore by Tempo Traveller

Marudamalai Temple

One of the most visited temples in Coimbatore. Situated on a hillock on the outskirts of the city. Dedicated to Lord Murugan. Best visited early morning before crowds build up. Easy to combine with other Coimbatore sightseeing spots on a full day local tour.

Dhyanalinga Isha Yoga Centre

A powerful spiritual destination just 30 km from Coimbatore city. Built by Sadhguru, this unique energy consecrated space is popular with family groups and corporate teams looking for a meaningful half day visit. Open to people of all backgrounds.

Siruvani Waterfalls

Around 37 km from Coimbatore. One of the most scenic natural spots in the region with water considered among the sweetest in the world. Best visited between June and November when water flow is at its peak. Popular with college groups and family outings.

VOC Park and Zoo

Great for family groups travelling with children. Centrally located in Coimbatore city and easy to cover in 2 to 3 hours. One of the most visited city attractions for families combining a Coimbatore local tour with quality time together.

Gedee Car Museum

A unique attraction in Coimbatore. Houses a rare collection of vintage cars and motorcycles from around the world. Worth including for groups with an interest in history and automobiles. A distinctive stop that most visitors have not seen before.

Black Thunder Theme Park

Popular with college groups and families. Around 40 km from Coimbatore city in Mettupalayam. Best booked as a full day outing. One of the largest water theme parks in South India and a favourite group outing destination from Coimbatore.

Anamalai Tiger Reserve

Around 85 km from Coimbatore. Perfect for wildlife and nature-loving groups. The reserve is home to elephants, leopards, and diverse bird species. Safari bookings recommended in advance. Popular corporate and family outing from Coimbatore.

Velliangiri Mountains

Known as the Kailash of the South. Popular pilgrimage and trekking destination around 40 km from Coimbatore. The seven-hill circuit is considered a powerful spiritual journey. Popular with spiritual groups and trekking teams from Coimbatore.
