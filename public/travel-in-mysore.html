Places to Visit in Mysore by Tempo Traveller

Mysore Palace (Amba Vilas Palace)

The most visited tourist attraction in Mysore. One of the grandest palace structures in India with stunning Indo-Saracenic architecture. The illuminated palace on Sunday evenings and during Dasara is one of the most spectacular sights in South India. Best visited on a full day city tour by tempo traveller.

Chamundi Hills and Chamundeshwari Temple

A 1,000-step climb or short drive to the hilltop temple dedicated to Goddess Chamundeshwari. Offers stunning panoramic views of Mysore city. Popular for early morning visits. The tempo traveller drops the group at the bottom of the steps or directly at the hilltop parking area depending on route preference.

Brindavan Gardens and KRS Dam

One of the most visited gardens in South India. Located about 19 km from Mysore at the foot of the Krishnaraja Sagara Dam. Famous for the musical fountain show held in the evenings. Best visited in the afternoon and evening as part of a full day Mysore sightseeing tour.

Srirangapatna

Around 16 km from Mysore. Historic island fort city with Ranganathaswamy Temple, Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace, and Gumbaz Mausoleum. A compact and fascinating heritage stop easily added to any Mysore sightseeing itinerary as a half day trip.

Karanji Lake and Nature Park

A nature reserve and bird sanctuary right in the heart of Mysore city. Home to over 170 bird species, a butterfly park, and a large aviary. A great stop for family groups travelling with children who want a quiet natural experience in Mysore.

Somnathpur Hoysala Temple

Around 38 km from Mysore. One of the finest examples of Hoysala architecture in Karnataka. The Keshava Temple at Somnathpur is a UNESCO World Heritage candidate with exquisite sculptural detail. Popular heritage stop for groups combining Mysore with a deeper Karnataka history tour.

Bandipur and Nagarhole Tiger Reserve

Around 80 km from Mysore. Two of the finest tiger reserves in India located back to back on the Kerala-Karnataka border. Safari bookings recommended in advance. Popular full day and overnight wildlife group outings from Mysore by tempo traveller.

Melukote Cheluvanarayana Temple

Around 51 km from Mysore. One of the most sacred Vaishnava pilgrimage sites in Karnataka. The Cheluvanarayana Temple atop a rocky hill is deeply significant for devotees. Popular religious group tour from Mysore combining Srirangapatna and Melukote in one day.
